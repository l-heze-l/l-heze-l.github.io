LUMA

LUMA (or Long-Range Universal Mission Administrator) is a extracurrical project in the Space Hardware Club of the University of Alabama in Huntsville.

The projects goal is to develop a BalloonSat system of modular payloads that would make high-altitude ballooning projects within the club and outside the club much more accessible.

The project has quite a few nuances, so if you want to learn more, please take a look at our most recent
Design Review.

I am the electrical sub-team lead of the project, putting a lot of the circuitry and power design into my responsibility.
